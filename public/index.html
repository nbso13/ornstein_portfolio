Bio

I was born and grew up outside Boston, MA, and completed my BSc. in Neuroscience at the University of Chicago in 2021.

After school, hoping to continue to build my research chops and learn more about my family's life in Europe post-war as survivors, I earned a Fulbright and moved to Munich Germany. On the research side, I worked in Professor Albrecht Schmidt's lab under Professor Lewis Chuang. I then did some data work on a Texas House campaign via Bluebonnet Data back stateside, before starting a Data Analyst role at Amazon Germany in December 2022. In July 2024, I decided I was ready to explore new opportunities and prepare to apply to graduate programs, and moved back to the Boston area.
